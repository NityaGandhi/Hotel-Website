About Us

Welcome to our family-owned motel, where comfort, hospitality, and a slower pace of life come together. We offer clean, comfortable rooms, friendly service, and a convenient location for travelers passing through or exploring the area.

Unlike a large hotel chain, our motel provides a quiet, personal experience in the heart of rural Sussex County. Surrounded by farmland, growing crops, and local industries, you'll get a glimpse of small-town Virginia and the people who call it home.

Our motel is located near Waverly, Virginia—known as the "Town of Peanuts." The surrounding area is part of the region where the first known commercial peanut crop in the United States was planted in 1842, making it an important part of American agricultural history. Today, visitors can enjoy the peaceful countryside, nearby local businesses, and the welcoming atmosphere that makes our community unique.

Whether you're here for work, visiting family, or simply looking for a relaxing place to stay, we're happy to welcome you and hope you enjoy your time with us.
